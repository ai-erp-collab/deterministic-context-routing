# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want participation in this project to be a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Assume good faith before assuming bad faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.

## Scope

This applies within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and
in any other space where someone is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the contact address
listed in `LICENSE`. Reports will be reviewed and may result in a warning,
temporary restriction, or permanent ban from project spaces, depending on
severity.
